About Me

I am Kaiwen Chen, or you can call me Kevin. I come from Shanghai, China and I am 19 years old right now. I study at NYU, IDM program. Here, I have a space to show my skills and what I like to do in digital art field. Besides my academics, I am good at photography, videography and editing. I also tried and had practical experiences in AR/VR, 3D Modeling, Game Desigm, Graphic Design and real-life designing during my Internship and my freshman year.

My dream is to create my own studio and publicize Chinese traditional art style by integrating modern digital art to the world.
