Brooklyn Bridge, 2019.11

Shot by Sony A7M3 + 28-70 f3.5-4.8 @ APSC Crop Mode

After a whole night writing essay for my expository writing class, me and my friend Karl was too tired to get some sleep. Instead sleeping in the Bobst library or going back to dormitary, we decided to walk to Brooklyn Bridge and wait for the sunrise.

The dawn comes ealier than we thought. When we arrived at city hall, the sun is already starting to rise. We rushed to the Bridge and took numerous amazing pictures. The sky line of downtown and midtown the Manhattan Bridge and also the Easter river.

When we walk till the end of the Brooklyn Bridge, Karl suddenly called me and let me see the Moon between the buildings. The composition was perfect. But my lens is not that telephoto so I used crop mode and finally get this amazing picture.
